# Datasets:
- Melbourne Wastewater - Daily Volume Received by Melbourne Water
- Melbourne Wastewater Treatment (East) - Daily Grid Electricity Consumption
- Wastewater Inlet - Daily Raw Water Quality - Eastern Treatment Plant (ETP)
- Wastewater Inlet - Hourly Flow - Eastern Treatment Plant (ETP)
- Wastewater Outlet - Daily Treated Water Quality - Eastern Treatment Plant (ETP)
- Water Supply - Daily Rainfall (at the 4 Major Harvesting Storage Dams)
- Water Supply - Daily Streamflow (into Melbourne's 4 Major Harvesting Storage Dams)
- Water Supply - Daily Volume Drawn from storage dams operated by Melbourne Water
- Water Supply - Daily Volume observed for storage dams operated by Melbourne Water
- Water Supply Storage Dams (Major System Storage)
