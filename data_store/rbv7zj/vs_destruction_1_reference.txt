Typical volatile solids (VS) destruction ranges for municipal anaerobic digesters:

Many wastewater engineering references report typical VS destruction for anaerobic digestion in the
range of ~45% to 60% for well-operated municipal full-scale digesters. Example source:

Metcalf & Eddy (4th Ed.) / 'Wastewater Engineering: Treatment and Resource Recovery' — Section on
Anaerobic Digestion and Sludge Stabilization. This textbook discusses expected VS destruction
in the typical range of about 40–60% depending on digester type and operating conditions.

Another practical guidance: U.S. EPA and technical manuals on biosolids and anaerobic digestion
commonly reference similar ranges for mesophilic digesters under normal loading and retention times.

Please consult the cited textbook or local design guidance for precise design/operational targets.
